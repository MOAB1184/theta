About Scout Accelerator

Scout Accelerator was created by scouts, for scouts. We understand the challenges of tracking advancement, managing troops, and staying organized in today's busy world. Our platform simplifies scouting administration while keeping the focus on fun and learning.

Whether you're a Scout working toward Eagle, a Scoutmaster managing your troop, or a patrol leader organizing activities, Scout Accelerator provides the tools you need to succeed. We believe scouting should be accessible to all, which is why our platform will always be completely free.
